Akira Toriyama

A timeline

  • 1955 Born on April 5 1955, Nagoya Japan
  • ~1970 drawing anime and manga early on and got recognition for his skills at a local art studio
  • ~1975 Designed posters for an advertising agency
  • ~1977 Submitted work to Shonen Jump Magazine for a contest: Awawa World 1977 and Mysterious Rain Jack 1978, either not winning an award. However, Kazuhiko Torishima encouraged him to continue.
  • 1978 Akira remained focus and published his debut Wonder Island, with Shonen Jump Magazine - issue 52
  • 1979 still achieving minor success he followed up with Tomato, Girl Detective
  • 1980 Published Dr.Slump with which he saw success
  • 1980-1984Continued with his latest manga with Shonen Jump Magazine, publishing 18 tankobon volumes
  • 1981-1982Recognized and awarded the best Shonen Jump manga artist for Pola & Roid & Pink
  • 1982 got married to Nachi Mikami- a manga artist of her own right. Often referred to as "Yoshimi"
  • 1982Dr.Slump recieved two awards: Shogakukan Manga Aard; the best series of the year
  • 1981-1986Toei Animation took Dr.Slump for tv adaptations on Fuji Tv, spanning 243 episodes; called Dr.Slump and Arale-Chan
  • 1983Started his own working studio called Bird Studio. During the same year, published Dragon boy and The Adventures of Tongpoo
  • 1984-1995Akira created Dragon Ball saga, spanning 42 tankobon volumes to movies and tv adaptations
  • 1997-1999Dr.Slump was brought back as a remake for 74 episodes
  • 2007Toriyama made a short sequel for Dr.Slump, renamed as Dr.Mashirito and Abale-chan
